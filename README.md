The Openstreetmap Bangladesh grown and evolved over time, but to stay relevant, active, and inclusive, we are planning to **reorganize the community structure**. This document will guide the process‚Äîfrom collecting initial feedback from key members to launching a full community survey and implementing new strategies.

We aim to:
- Identify gaps and opportunities
- Understand member needs and motivations
- Strengthen collaboration, visibility, and local chapters
- Empower contributors through training, leadership, and better communication
